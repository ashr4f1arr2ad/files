Interior design
Description

We design interiors with documentation  that  is ready for construction  and  can be submitted for DA approval. The design concept is integrated into the interior  space  through  our  design.

Our designs will convert your workplace from  just a place where you  do business to an environment that shares your DNA. The design concept is implemented so that the workspace is completely your own. It shares your culture. It reflects your brand.

  • 1. Living room: Sofa, wine cabinet, bookshelf, ceiling lamp, etc
  • 2. Bedroom: bed, wardrobe, dressing table, night lamp
  • 3. Kitchen: cupboard, kitchen table, dining table, corner cabinet
  • 4. Working room: bookcase, desk, table lamp, tree stand, etc
  • 5. Room for small children: Bed, bookshelf, desk

Our designs find the perfect balance between your needs, creativity and practicality.  Many  interior  designers focus on over the top designs that are costly – but the resulting interiors are often impractical to use. Other  interior design  companies are at the other extreme, designing generic interiors that are practical and simple to fit out, but the interior does not reflect your business.
